Ecological Collapse




Up to a million species could go extinct, if humanity doesn't force goverments and industries to clean up their act.



The prediction comes from a summary of an upcoming United Nations biodiversity repot. Though scientists have long warned us about humanity's impact on plant and animal species, the Global Assesment Report on Biodiversity and Ecosystem Services is one of the most sweeping(and starling) on the state of the planet's natural systems, based on thousands of scientific studies and athored by hundreds of international experts.

(Lindwall 2019)
